This website serves as the home for International Studies Quarterly as well as, among other things, a virtual forum for the scholarly discussion of articles published in the journal, and the issues that they raise. Published ISQ articles have associated posts here on the website. We also feature online symposia discussing particular articles and issues. Registered users can join in the conversation in the comments section of those posts.

For information about submitting to the journal, see our guidelines and policies.

Representing 80 countries, ISA has over 6,500 members worldwide and is the most respected and widely known scholarly association in this field. Endeavoring to create communities of scholars dedicated to international studies, ISA is divided into 6 geographic subdivisions of ISA (Regions), 28 thematic groups (Sections) and 3 Caucuses which provide opportunities to exchange ideas and research with local colleagues and within specific subject areas.
